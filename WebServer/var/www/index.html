Mozart's Lacrimosa

Lacrimosa is a powerful and emotional piece of music composed by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. It is part of his Requiem Mass in D minor, K. 626. Lacrimosa means "weeping" or "tearful", and the music evokes a sense of sorrow and mourning.

Listen to Lacrimosa and experience the beauty and depth of Mozart's music:

Lyrics

Lacrimosa dies illa,
Qua resurget ex favilla
Judicandus homo reus.
Huic ergo parce, Deus,
Pie Jesu Domine,
Dona eis requiem. Amen.

Translation:
That day of tears,
On which shall rise from the ashes
Guilty man to be judged.
Therefore spare him, O God,
Merciful Lord Jesus,
Grant them rest. Amen.
